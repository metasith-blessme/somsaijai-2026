For each daily report, the following data has been organized:

1. DATE & TIME INFORMATION
   - Calendar date (DD.MM.YYYY format)
   - Day of week (Sunday - Saturday)

2. PRODUCT SALES (Each product line has Cash and Scan/Transfer amounts):
   
   a) Orange Juice Cups
      - Cup(80) - 80 baht: Cash | Scan
      - Cup(60) - 60 baht: Cash | Scan
      
   b) Bottles
      - Bot(40) - 40 baht: Cash | Scan
      - Bot(200) - 200 baht: Cash | Scan
      
   c) Standard Cups
      - Cup(100) - 100 baht: Cash | Scan
      - Cup(50) - 50 baht: Cash | Scan
      
   d) Watermelon Drinks
      - Watermelon(50) - 50 baht: Cash | Scan
      - Watermelon(60) - 60 baht: Cash | Scan
      
   e) Seasonal/Special Items
      - Mango(60) - 60 baht: Cash | Scan (appears on some days)
      - Coconut(60) - 60 baht: Cash | Scan (appears on some days)

3. DAILY SUMMARY
   - Total Revenue (All →): Sum of all product sales
   - Total Cash Collected (Cash →): All cash payments received
   - Expenses (Ice/expenses →): Operating costs (primarily ice)
   - Net Cash (Total cash after expenses): Cash minus expenses
   - Total Scan/Transfer (Scan →): Electronic payment total

4. INVENTORY TRACKING
   
   Cups Sold by Type:
   - Orange juice cups (number of units)
   - Watermelon cups (number of units)
   - Mango cups (number of units, if applicable)
   - Total cups (combined count)
   
   Bottles:
   - Total bottles sold (combined 40 baht and 200 baht)

5. MATERIALS USED (bottom left of each report)
   - Orange (number of baskets consumed)
   - Watermelon (number of pieces used)
   - Other items as recorded
