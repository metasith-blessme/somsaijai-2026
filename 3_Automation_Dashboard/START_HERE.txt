================================================================================
KEY THINGS TO REMEMBER
================================================================================

✓ IMAGE NUMBERS ≠ DATES
  Image 1 might be February 3rd, image 2 might be February 1st
  Always read the actual date from each image

✓ TWO PAYMENT METHODS
  Each product has: Cash amount AND Scan/Transfer amount
  Don't mix them up - use separate columns

✓ SOME PRODUCTS MAY BE MISSING
  Mango(60) and Coconut(60) don't appear every day
  Leave blank if not in that day's image

✓ CURRENCY IS BAHT
  All amounts are Thai Baht (฿)
  No need to convert

✓ TOTALS SHOULD MATCH
  Total Revenue = Sum of all product sales
  Cash + Scan should roughly equal Total Revenue
  (They may not match exactly due to discounts/adjustments)

✓ CAREFUL WITH HANDWRITING
  Numbers may be unclear
  Cross-reference with other numbers to verify
  Example: If total is 1000 baht, check that products add up close

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Do I need to match images to calendar dates?
A: Yes. The image numbers (1-28) don't match February calendar dates.
   Always read the date written on each image.

Q: What if a product is missing from an image?
A: Leave it blank in the CSV. Not every product appears every day.

Q: Should I enter zeroes for missing products?
A: No, leave blank. Zeroes mean the product sold zero units, which is 
   different from "not recorded."

Q: What if numbers are hard to read?
A: Do your best. Cross-check with other columns if available.
   Ask if you're really unsure.

Q: Can I use Google Sheets instead of Excel?
A: Yes! The CSV file works with both. Google Sheets can import CSV files.

Q: What if I make a mistake?
A: Just correct it. The spreadsheet is editable.

Q: How long does this take?
A: 2-4 hours depending on handwriting clarity and your speed.

Q: What do I do when finished?
A: Save the CSV file. You can then import to Excel, database, or analysis tool.

Q: Can I extract just some days?
A: Yes, you can do it in sections. Just save your progress.
